Why Invest in FantomRise?

Unlike a burn from non-circulating supply, the buyback immediately transfers value to the token holders and the community by purchasing tokens from the open market to burn.

With FantomRise’s unique buyback function, FRS tokens are bought back from the DeFi market, resulting in an immediate increase in price. Those tokens are then instantly burned, permanently removing them from the circulating supply.

As all of these tokens were on the market, being bought and sold, this creates a true supply constriction which makes FantomRise Hyper-Deflationary.

FantomRise token holders also benefit through a 2% static reflection reward from every transaction.

The Buyback Function

The Kraken is the biggest whale of all, and acts as a safety net for all FantomRise holders.

The FantomRise buyback function, also known as The Kraken, is funded by a 7% strategic buyback fee. The tokens collected from the fees are converted into FTM and securely locked and stored in the FantomRise contract. These funds are known as the Kraken’s Strategic Reserves.

These reserves then become independent of the price of FRS. The Strategic Reserves ready to be deployed stand at FTM ($ Million USDT). The contract is coded so that the FTM in the Kraken’s Strategic Reserves cannot be withdrawn and can only be utilized for the purposes of buying back FRS coins from the open market and burning them.

When the buyback function is turned on, the contract automatically buys back and burns tokens after every sell transaction.

Once the FRS tokens are bought back, the new FTM amount is added to the liquidity pool ($ Million USDT of FTM) and the FRS tokens bought are immediately burned. % of the circulating supply has been burnt so far.

This creates a true burn and guarantees the price per token will increase every time the buyback is activated.

The Kraken is deployed strategically at specific moments to allow FantomRise to maintain a stable price floor during downward market trends, chart manipulation, and whale dumps, securing the long-term success of the project.

The FantomRise Protocol
